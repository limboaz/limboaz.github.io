About Me

I started programming when I transferred to Stony Brook University in January 2017. It has not been a long time till now, but things work like a magic click that I have found my passion, and decided to go for a research career in system-related fields.

I am currently working on fsbench and hpcperf at Filesystems and Storage Lab (FSL) at Stony Brook University, supervised by Prof. Erez Zadok, sponsored by NSF.

Prior to joining FSL, I have worked on mobile computing with Prof. Fan Ye at CEWIT. I also spent a summer at Tencent as an SDE intern.
